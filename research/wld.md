# WLD — Worldcoin (World)

**asset_id**: `wld`
**Current classification**: sector_code=`App Utilities (4)`, chain_ecosystem=`ETH`
**Current sector cohesion**: -0.018, NEG (parent sector)

## What it is

Worldcoin (rebranded "World") is a proof-of-personhood / biometric-identity
network. Users scan their iris via "Orb" hardware to mint a World ID,
used as Sybil-resistant identity primitive. Token is distributed to
verified humans and used for governance/ecosystem incentives.

## Value accrual mechanism

WLD has scheduled emission to verified humans (UBI-style airdrop) plus
operational allocations. The token is governance over the protocol and a
medium for in-app payments (World App). No fee burn; value accrual is
demand-driven (apps requiring World ID, partnerships) against significant
ongoing emission. Heavy float unlock schedule remains a key supply factor.
