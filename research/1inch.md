## What it is

1inch is a DEX aggregator that routes swaps across many AMMs (Uniswap, Curve, Balancer, etc.) plus its own Pathfinder algorithm and Fusion intent-based execution. It is deployed on Ethereum and most major EVM chains. The protocol also operates an MEV-resistant resolver auction system (Fusion / Fusion+).

## Value accrual mechanism

1INCH holders can stake into the Aggregation Protocol / Fusion to earn a share of resolver fees and protocol revenue (the "Unicorn Power" system). Value capture depends on volume routed through 1inch and adoption of Fusion. Without a direct fee switch on basic swaps, accrual is largely governance + Fusion-resolver economics.

## Economic siblings

JUP (Solana DEX aggregator — closest functional sibling), UNI/CRV/AERO/CAKE (underlying AMMs aggregated by 1inch). JUP and 1INCH are arguably their own micro-cohort within DEX: aggregators rather than liquidity venues.
