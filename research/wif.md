## What it is

dogwifhat is a Solana SPL meme launched November 2023, named after the
"dog wearing a hat" internet image. It became the leading Solana meme by
market cap in 2024 alongside BONK, and one of the iconic memes of the
post-FTX Solana revival.

## Value accrual mechanism

Fixed supply (~999 M). No staking, no burn, no protocol revenue. Pure
attention-driven Solana meme; gas paid in SOL accrues to Solana, not WIF.
