## What it is

Plasma is a stablecoin-focused L1 built from the ground up for USDT
payments. Launched mainnet beta September 25, 2025 with $2B+ TVL on
day one. Zero-fee USDT transfers via a custom consensus (PlasmaBFT)
with EVM compatibility. Backed by Tether (Paolo Ardoino), Founders
Fund, and Framework Ventures; public sale raised ~$373M.
[coindesk.com/markets/2025/09/25/stablecoin-focused-plasma-s-xpl-token-debuts, theblock.co/post/372300]

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking — XPL secures consensus, (2) gas for complex
transactions (simple USDT sends are gasless, but contract deployment
and DeFi requires XPL or converted USDT-as-fee), (3) ecosystem
incentives (40% supply allocated to ecosystem growth). Economic
identity is the closest pure-play "USDT settlement L1" alongside TRON
— but XPL itself is much smaller and earlier-stage. 10B total supply,
significant unlock overhang from team (25%) and investors (25%).
