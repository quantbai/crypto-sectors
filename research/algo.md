## What it is

Algorand is a non-EVM PoS L1 founded by Silvio Micali (Turing Award),
launched 2019. Pure PoS with a VRF-based consensus (no slashing).
TEAL/PyTeal smart contracts. Historically pitched as institutional /
CBDC-grade infrastructure.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Transaction fees paid in ALGO (minimum 0.001 ALGO/tx), mostly
recycled to participants, (2) governance rewards historically (now
deprecated), (3) validator/relay-node rewards. Value accrual is
extremely weak: on-chain activity is minimal, no fee burn, large
foundation supply overhang. Returns dominated by retail narrative and
foundation marketing.
