## What it is

NEAR is a non-EVM PoS L1 with sharding (Nightshade), launched 2020.
Native Rust/AssemblyScript runtime. Hosts the Aurora EVM as a sub-chain
for EVM compatibility. Pivoted heavily into AI-agent narrative (NEAR
AI, chain abstraction) in 2024-25.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) 70% of transaction fees burned, 30% to contract developers
("gas rebates"), (2) validator staking yield from ~5% inflation,
(3) demand from AI-agent / chain-abstraction narrative is largely
speculative rather than fee-driven. Fundamentals are middling: real
fee revenue is low, but the burn mechanic is structurally sound.
