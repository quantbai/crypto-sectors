## What it is

DoubleZero is a high-performance networking DePIN providing dedicated
fiber connectivity between blockchain validators (initially Solana).
Replaces the public internet for validator-to-validator traffic via
multicast and edge filtration. Mainnet October 2025; ~22% of staked SOL
plugged in. Founded by ex-Solana Foundation Austin Federa.
Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2025/10/01/doublezero-mainnet-goes-live-with-nearly-21-of-staked-sol-on-board).

## Value accrual mechanism

2Z (SPL token on Solana) pays for: (1) bandwidth/data routing services,
(2) rewards to fiber-infrastructure providers (validators + fiber
contributors like Jump, Galaxy, RockawayX, Jito). Validator-fee demand
underpins token. Inflationary early via contributor rewards; long-term
relies on real fiber-network usage. Source: [Phemex](https://phemex.com/academy/what-is-doublezero-2z).
