## What it is

Arweave is a permanent-storage protocol: users pay once to store data
"forever" via an endowment model. Uses Succinct Proofs of Random Access
(SPoRA) consensus. Hosts Solana ledger archives, NFT metadata, and AO
("Arweave Compute") — a hyper-parallel compute layer launched 2024.

## Value accrual mechanism

Users pay AR upfront for storage; most of that AR is locked in an
endowment that pays out to miners over decades to incentivize replication.
Block reward + endowment payout funds miners. AO compute and Arweave-as-DA
(data availability) add fee-demand sources. No burn; supply growth slows
as endowment funds payouts.
