## What it is

Morpho is a lending protocol that started as a P2P matching layer on top of Aave/Compound and evolved into Morpho Blue — a minimal, isolated-market lending primitive on which third parties build curated vaults (MetaMorpho). It is deployed on Ethereum, Base, and other EVM chains. By 2025 Morpho became one of the largest DeFi lending venues by TVL.

## Value accrual mechanism

The MORPHO token activated transferability in late 2024. Governance directs protocol revenue (vault fees, accrual on bad-debt insurance), with current accrual mostly through DAO treasury. Active discussion around a fee switch on Morpho Blue. Value driver: lending TVL growth and MetaMorpho vault adoption.

## Economic siblings

AAVE, COMP, FLUID (Ethereum lending). Closest mechanically: AAVE (same product, similar curated-vault offerings via Aave). KMNO and JST as cross-chain lending peers.
