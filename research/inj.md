## What it is

Injective is a Cosmos-SDK-based L1 with native order-book infrastructure
("DeFi-native" exchange primitives at the chain level), CosmWasm smart
contracts, and an Ethereum-VM rollup (inEVM). Founded by Eric Chen /
Albert Chon (Injective Labs), backed by Binance Labs / Pantera.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) INJ buy-back-and-burn auction: 60% of dApp fee revenue across the
chain is auctioned weekly, with the proceeds burned (INJ 3.0
tokenomics — aggressive deflationary mechanic), (2) validator staking
yield (~10%), (3) governance. The burn-auction is one of the most
direct fee-to-token value-accrual mechanisms in the universe — closer
to Aave / Maker buyback than to a typical L1 fee burn.
