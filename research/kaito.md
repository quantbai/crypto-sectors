## What it is

Kaito is an AI-powered "InfoFi" (information finance) platform indexing
crypto info (X, Discord, governance forums, podcasts) via LLMs, ranking
attention and content. Founded 2022 by ex-Citadel quant Yu Hu. Original
"Yaps" post-to-earn program sunset Jan 2026 (X API access revoked);
pivoted to Kaito Pro/Studio/Markets, Polymarket-partnered Attention
Markets. Source: [CoinGecko Learn](https://www.coingecko.com/learn/what-is-kaito-earn-yap-points).

## Value accrual mechanism

KAITO is used for platform payments, governance, and tokenized attention/
information markets. Listed Feb 2025 on major exchanges. Revenue from
Pro subscriptions, Studio brand campaigns, and Markets fees flows to
DAO/treasury. Value accrual is hybrid: SaaS-like (Pro) + attention-market
GMV. No public buy-and-burn confirmed.
