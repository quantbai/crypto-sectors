## What it is

Polkadot is a multi-chain "L0" relay-chain network (live 2020) hosting
parachains that share security from the relay. Designed by Gavin Wood
(Ethereum co-founder); the architecture switched from parachain auctions
to Agile Coretime (2024) selling block-space directly.

## Value accrual mechanism

DOT is staked to secure the relay chain (~14% nominal staking yield;
~50% supply staked). Issuance is uncapped, currently ~7% inflation but
reduced under the JAM roadmap. DOT is also used to buy Coretime from the
relay тАФ direct fee channel for parachain demand. Governance is on-chain
via OpenGov.
