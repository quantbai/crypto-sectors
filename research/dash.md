## What it is

Dash (formerly Darkcoin / XCoin, 2014) is a BTC codebase fork pioneering
masternode-based governance, optional CoinJoin-style privacy (PrivateSend),
and near-instant settlement (InstantSend). Marketed primarily as digital
cash for payments, with privacy as a secondary feature.

## Value accrual mechanism

PoW with ~18.9 M near-cap, slow decay issuance. Block rewards split
~45% miners / 45% masternodes / 10% treasury. Masternodes require 1,000
DASH collateral — supply-lock mechanic is real but doesn't produce
external revenue. Value is dominated by payment-corridor (LatAm) demand
plus privacy-coin regulatory beta.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- ZEN — Zcash sibling

- XRP, XLM — payment-rail peers

## Classification verdict

- **Keep current** (102020 Privacy Coins) with the caveat that DASH is a
  privacy-light + payment hybrid. Best sub-group: "Privacy" with ZEC/ZEN.
- Rationale: DASH's privacy feature is opt-in CoinJoin, much weaker than
  Zcash's zk-SNARKs, so it's less of a "pure privacy" play. But its
  delisting-risk beta clusters it with ZEC/ZEN; this dominates return
  drivers in recent years.
