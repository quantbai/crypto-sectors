## What it is

EigenLayer is the foundational restaking protocol on Ethereum: ETH stakers/LST holders can opt-in to re-pledge their staked ETH as cryptoeconomic security for additional services (AVSs — actively validated services) such as data availability layers, oracles, bridges, and rollup sequencers. The 2025 rebrand "EigenCloud" expanded the product into a verifiable-compute platform. EIGEN is the protocol's native token, used as additional slashable collateral for intersubjective faults.

## Value accrual mechanism

EIGEN is dual-use: (a) restakable alongside ETH to secure AVSs, with stakers earning protocol incentives + AVS fees, and (b) governance over the EigenLayer protocol parameters. Value accrual depends on AVS adoption and fee flows paid in EIGEN/ETH. Treated as an "intersubjective slashing" asset for fault-resolution outside Ethereum's protocol consensus.

## Economic siblings
