## What it is

TON (The Open Network) is a non-EVM L1 originally designed by Telegram,
now developed by the TON Foundation. Tightly integrated with Telegram's
~900M MAU messaging app for in-app wallet, mini-apps, and stablecoin
payments (USDT-TON launched 2024). Architecture is sharded BFT with
TVM (TON Virtual Machine).

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking yield, (2) transaction fees (paid in TON, with
partial burn), (3) demand from Telegram-native flows: USDT transfers,
mini-app payments, Notcoin / Hamster-style retail social trading.
Value accrual is driven by Telegram's user funnel — closer to a "social
platform L1" than a developer-platform L1.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- TRX — also stablecoin-settlement-driven, captive user base, defensive flow profile
- SOL — retail/social-trading flow (memecoin parallel to Notcoin/tap-to-earn)
- Less so: ETH/AVAX (developer-platform L1s)

## Classification verdict

- **Keep current** sector, sub-group as "Captive-distribution L1" — TON's economic identity is "Telegram's blockchain" more than "general-purpose SCP". Closer economic profile to TRX (captive USDT distribution) than to ETH/SOL.
- Rationale: returns are likely to co-move with Telegram-narrative events (Pavel Durov news, TON Foundation announcements) more than generic L1 beta.
