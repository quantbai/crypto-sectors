## What it is

ApeCoin is the ERC-20 governance/utility token of the BAYC / Yuga Labs
ecosystem (Bored Ape Yacht Club, Mutant Apes, Otherside metaverse).
Launched March 2022 by the ApeCoin DAO. As of June 2025, governance is
transitioning to a centralized "ApeCo" entity under Yuga CEO Greg Solano.

## Value accrual mechanism

1 B fixed supply with multi-year unlock schedule (Yuga, launch
contributors, DAO treasury). Used for: BAYC ecosystem governance,
Otherside parcel transactions, Benji Bananas play-and-earn, ApeChain L3
gas (ApeChain is an Arbitrum Orbit L3 launched 2024). Staking program
(Horizen-style ApeStake) was wound down 2024. Real demand driver is NFT-
floor cycles for BAYC and ecosystem product launches.
