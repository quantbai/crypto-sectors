## What it is

Kite is an EVM-compatible PoS L1 (deployed as Avalanche L1 / "Kite Chain")
billed as "the payments layer for the AI agent economy" — programmable
identity (Agent Passport), stablecoin settlement rails, and service
discovery for autonomous AI agents. Mainnet 2026. Source: [gokite.ai](https://gokite.ai/).

## Value accrual mechanism

KITE (10B max supply) is utility + staking token: stakers secure the L1,
agents/services pay protocol fees in KITE (transitioning to stablecoins
over time per whitepaper), and KITE gates specific agent activities.
Inflationary early (validator rewards in KITE), planned shift to stablecoin
fees as adoption grows. Value accrual is bet on agent-economy transaction
volume on Kite Chain.
