## What it is

Zcash is a BTC-codebase fork (2016) implementing zk-SNARK shielded
transactions via the Sapling/Orchard upgrades. The shielded pool is the
canonical optional-privacy design in crypto. ECC and Zcash Foundation
are primary development orgs.

## Value accrual mechanism

PoW with a 21 M hard cap and BTC-style halving schedule (next: 2028). A
Dev Fund (formerly Founders' Reward) currently takes 20% of issuance for
core development. No staking (NU6 PoS transition is on roadmap but not
shipped). Value is store-of-value + privacy-narrative demand. Returns
correlate with BTC beta and regulatory-pressure cycles on privacy coins
(e.g., 2023-25 CEX delistings drove negative spikes).
