## What it is

Pump.fun is the dominant Solana memecoin launchpad — anyone can deploy a bonding-curve memecoin in seconds. Tokens trade on the bonding curve until ~$69K market cap, then "graduate" to PumpSwap (Pump.fun's own AMM, launched 2025) or previously Raydium. The platform has launched 11M+ tokens and generated >$800M revenue since Jan 2024, with periodic dominance of all Solana token launches [Phemex](https://phemex.com/academy/what-is-pump-fun-solana-meme-coin-launchpad-pump-token).

## Value accrual mechanism

PUMP launched via a $1.3B ICO in July 2025 (FDV $4B). The token funds protocol buybacks from Pump.fun creator/swap-fee revenue and serves as governance/utility for the ecosystem. Highly cyclical revenue tied to Solana retail memecoin volume; revenue dropped sharply during memecoin lulls and recovered during pumps. Behavior closer to a Solana-beta retail-flow proxy than a structural DeFi protocol.

## Economic siblings

Mechanically distinct from any other coin in our DeFi set. Closest functional analogues are memecoins themselves (PEPE, SHIB-style flow exposure) and possibly RAY (memecoin-trading venue). No direct peer in the 32-coin universe.
