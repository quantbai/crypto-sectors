## What it is

Sui is a non-EVM L1 built by Mysten Labs (ex-Meta Diem/Novi engineers),
launched May 2023. Object-centric data model with parallel execution
using the Move VM. Differentiated against Aptos (the other Move chain)
by the object-DAG architecture vs Aptos's account model.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking yield from inflation, (2) gas fees with a portion
flowing to a storage fund (long-term cost coverage rather than burn),
(3) on-chain DeFi (Cetus, NAVI, Suilend) and an active memecoin /
consumer-app ecosystem. Sui has captured meaningful retail trading
flow in 2024-25, more than Aptos.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- APT — direct sibling (Move VM, ex-Meta-Diem team) — closest economic peer
- SOL — also non-EVM monolithic chain targeting retail / consumer apps
- Less so: ETH/AVAX (EVM)
