## What it is

$TRUMP is a Solana SPL meme launched January 17, 2025 — three days before
Donald Trump's second inauguration. Officially endorsed by Trump on X /
Truth Social. 1 B total supply; 800 M held by two Trump-affiliated
entities (CIC Digital, Fight Fight Fight LLC), 200 M floated.

## Value accrual mechanism

Fixed supply with aggressive insider unlock schedule (40 M tokens
unlocked at month-3, ~200 M held by insiders with multi-year vesting).
No staking, no burn, no protocol revenue. Massive trading-fee capture
went to insiders during launch (NYT/Fortune reporting: ~$100 M in fees
captured, ~$2 B in retail losses by Q1-2025). Value is pure political-
attention beta; vulnerable to insider-unlock supply shocks.
