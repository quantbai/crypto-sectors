## What it is

SKY is the rebrand of MakerDAO (MKR) under the "Endgame" plan, completed 2024–2025. MakerDAO is the original crypto-backed CDP (collateralized debt position) stablecoin issuer, minting DAI (now also USDS) against ETH, wstETH, RWA collateral, and other assets. SKY now serves as the governance + value-capture token at a 24,000:1 ratio to legacy MKR.

## Value accrual mechanism

The Sky/Maker DAO earns interest from CDP stability fees plus RWA portfolio yield (large allocations to tokenized treasuries via Monetalis, BlockTower, etc.) and Peg Stability Module (USDC swap) fees. Surplus revenue funds the Smart Burn Engine, which buys back and burns SKY (formerly MKR). SKY is therefore a buyback-deflation token whose cash flows resemble a stablecoin-issuer bank earning net interest margin on a CDP loan book + treasury portfolio.

## Economic siblings

Other stablecoin/CDP issuer governance tokens: ENA (Ethena, synthetic dollar issuer), RSR (Reserve, basket-backed dollar). Adjacent: AAVE via GHO (secondary).
