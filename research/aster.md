## What it is

Aster is a perp+spot DEX launched September 2025, formed by the merger of Astherus and APX Finance, backed by YZi Labs (Binance founders' family office) with CZ as an advisor. Deployed across BNB Chain, Ethereum, Solana, and Arbitrum. Offers up to 1001x leverage in Simple Mode, plus a Pro Mode with 24/7 stock perps, hidden orders, and grid trading. Accepts LSTs (asBNB) and yield-bearing stablecoins (USDF) as collateral. Plans its own L1 ("Aster Chain") [Coin Bureau](https://coinbureau.com/review/what-is-aster-crypto).

## Value accrual mechanism

ASTER is governance + ecosystem token (8B supply, BEP-20). 53.5% allocated to airdrops/community, 30% ecosystem. Stated utilities: governance, fee discounts, staking rewards, future buybacks funded by perp trading revenue. Highly cyclical with perp volume.

## Economic siblings
