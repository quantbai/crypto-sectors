## What it is

NEO ("Chinese Ethereum") is a non-EVM L1 originally launched as
Antshares in 2014, rebranded NEO in 2017. dBFT consensus, dual-token
model (NEO for governance / stake, GAS for fees). Once a top-10 chain
during the 2017 bull market; now a dormant legacy chain with minimal
DeFi activity. NEO N3 mainnet (2021) was a complete rewrite but did
not revive activity.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Holding NEO accrues GAS continuously (1:1 staking-like emission),
which is then used to pay for transactions, (2) NEO governance voting,
(3) no fee burn on NEO itself; GAS is the working token. Value
accrual is structurally weak: GAS emission is the main "yield" but
GAS itself has minimal demand because chain activity is near zero.
