## What it is

Ether.fi is the largest liquid restaking protocol on Ethereum. Users deposit ETH → receive eETH (rebasing) or weETH (wrapped) → Ether.fi stakes the ETH with node operators AND restakes it via EigenLayer + select AVSs, layering AVS yield on top of base ETH staking yield. The project also operates Cash (a self-custodial crypto debit card) and Liquid (curated yield vaults).

## Value accrual mechanism

ETHFI is the governance token. The protocol takes a fee on staking + restaking rewards; revenue accrues to the DAO treasury, with the Cash product additionally generating subscription/interchange revenue. Active discussions around buybacks and fee distribution; some emissions distributed via points/airdrop programs. Value driver: weETH float × restaking-yield spread.
