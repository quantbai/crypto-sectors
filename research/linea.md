## What it is

Linea is a zkEVM L2 on Ethereum developed by Consensys (MetaMask /
Infura), launched September 10, 2025 with a 72 B-supply token via a TGE
that allocated 9.36 B to airdrop claimants. Marketed as "silver to
Ethereum's gold".

## Value accrual mechanism

LINEA is NOT a governance token; strategic decisions are handled by the
Linea Consortium of aligned institutions. Value accrual is mechanical:
on each transaction, 20% of ETH-denominated network fees burn ETH at
protocol level, and 80% are used to buy and burn LINEA — a direct
deflationary fee channel. No team / VC allocation. 85% of supply allocated
to ecosystem and ecosystem fund.
