## What it is

Aptos is a non-EVM L1 built by Aptos Labs (ex-Meta Diem/Novi team),
launched October 2022. Uses the Move VM with an account model
(distinct from Sui's object DAG). Positioned as high-throughput
consumer-app and payments chain; less retail trading activity than Sui.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking yield from inflation, (2) gas fees paid to
validators (burn introduced via AIP-119 but minor in practice),
(3) on-chain DeFi (Thala, Aries, Echo) and stablecoin issuance
(USDt-Aptos). Value accrual mechanism nearly identical to SUI but with
materially lower on-chain activity in 2024-25.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- SUI — direct sibling (Move VM, ex-Meta team, similar unlock schedule)
- SOL — also non-EVM monolithic high-TPS chain
- Less so: ETH/AVAX (EVM)
