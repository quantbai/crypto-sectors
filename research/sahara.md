## What it is

Sahara AI is a full-stack AI-native blockchain platform splitting the AI
lifecycle (data, training, models, deployment) into onchain marketplaces.
Founded 2023 by USC professor Sean Ren + ex-Binance Labs Tyler Zhou.
Partners include Microsoft, Amazon, MIT, Snap. Source: [WEEX Crypto Wiki](https://www.weex.com/wiki/article/what-is-sahara-ai-sahara-token-explained-2026-guide-z6zw6yxs835ozm1n7m8efkoj).

## Value accrual mechanism

SAHARA powers (1) data-labeling rewards to contributors, (2) developer-
platform fees for training/deploying models, (3) onchain dataset/model
license payments. Token gates participation across the stack; circulating
supply ~3.3B against larger total. Inflationary via contributor rewards;
demand from enterprise/dev licensing. Value accrual depends on platform
GMV vs unlock schedule.
