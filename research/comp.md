## What it is

Compound is one of the original pool-based DeFi lending protocols on Ethereum (launched 2018, governance launched mid-2020). Users supply assets to earn variable interest and borrow against them. Compound v3 ("Comet") shifted to isolated USDC/USDT base markets with multiple collateral assets, and is deployed across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Polygon, and other EVM chains.

## Value accrual mechanism

COMP is primarily a governance token. Liquidity-mining rewards historically distributed COMP to suppliers and borrowers (this bootstrapped TVL in 2020-21). The DAO controls market parameters and treasury revenue (reserve-factor share of interest paid by borrowers). No direct fee-switch or buyback in place; value capture is governance optionality and treasury accumulation. Market share has eroded versus Aave and Morpho.
