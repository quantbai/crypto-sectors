## What it is

Arbitrum is the leading optimistic rollup on Ethereum (Offchain Labs)
and the dominant L2 by TVL. ARB is the DAO governance token, airdropped
March 2023; it does not currently capture sequencer fees.

## Value accrual mechanism

Sequencer revenue (~$25-30 M/yr net of L1 data costs post-EIP-4844)
flows to the Arbitrum Foundation, controlled by ARB-holder governance
via AIPs. Recent governance includes Timeboost auction revenue, stablecoin
program yields, and treasury management ‚Äî Q4 2025 run-rate ~$26 M. ARB
holders can direct revenue to buybacks, ecosystem grants, or burn but no
permanent fee-switch is yet active. So value accrual is currently
indirect (governance over treasury).
