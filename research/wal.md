## What it is

Walrus is a decentralized storage and data-availability protocol built
on Sui (Move ecosystem) by Mysten Labs / Walrus Foundation. Uses erasure
coding ("Red Stuff" protocol) to fragment files across storage nodes.
Mainnet launched March 27, 2025. Source: [Mysten Labs blog](https://www.mystenlabs.com/blog/announcing-the-official-walrus-whitepaper).

## Value accrual mechanism

WAL is the protocol token for: (1) storage payments by clients, (2)
delegated PoS staking by storage nodes (slashable for missing proofs),
(3) governance. Max supply 5B (community-heavy allocation; 30% core,
7% private investors). Storage demand → WAL fee revenue → staker rewards.
Source: [Mysten Labs](https://www.mystenlabs.com/blog/announcing-walrus-a-decentralized-storage-and-data-availability-protocol).
