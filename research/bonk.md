## What it is

BONK is a Solana SPL meme launched Christmas 2022 with a 50% airdrop to
the Solana community and ecosystem. It was the first major Solana meme
and is credited with helping revive Solana's developer momentum after
the FTX collapse.

## Value accrual mechanism

Total supply ~94 T. Periodic ecosystem burns (BONKburn, BONKbot fee
burns) provide a mild deflationary signal but volumes are small relative
to supply. Some genuine product surface — BONKbot Telegram trading bot
generates real fees in SOL, a fraction routed to BONK buybacks/burns.
Still primarily attention-driven; the BONKbot revenue makes BONK slightly
more "productive" than pure memes.
