## What it is

Cardano is a non-EVM proof-of-stake L1 designed around peer-reviewed
academic research (Ouroboros PoS, eUTXO model, Plutus smart contracts).
Smart contracts launched September 2021. Despite long history,
on-chain DeFi/TVL remains marginal vs ETH/SOL/peers.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Staking yield from inflation (~3-4%, delegated to stake pools — no
slashing, low friction), (2) transaction fees paid to validators (no
burn mechanism), (3) governance via Voltaire era / treasury. Value
accrual is weak: no fee burn, limited application revenue, and ADA's
price is largely driven by retail narrative / brand rather than
fundamental on-chain cash flow.
