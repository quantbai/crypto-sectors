## What it is

Qtum is a 2017-vintage hybrid chain that combines Bitcoin's UTXO model
with an EVM. PoS via "Mutualized Proof-of-Stake". Co-founded by
Patrick Dai. Once positioned as the "Bitcoin meets Ethereum" chain;
now a legacy chain with essentially zero active development or DeFi.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Block reward to stakers (PoS inflation), (2) transaction fees to
stakers, (3) no burn mechanism. Value accrual is effectively zero —
on-chain activity is negligible, no meaningful DeFi or applications.
Returns driven entirely by speculative beta to broader crypto cycles
and occasional China-narrative pumps.
