## What it is

Horizen is a Zcash fork (2017) that branched into a sidechain platform
(Zendoo) while retaining optional zk-SNARK shielded transactions on the
main chain. As of 2024-25 the project has pivoted toward zk-rollup
infrastructure ("Horizen 2.0" on the EON EVM sidechain).

## Value accrual mechanism

PoW with BTC-style 21 M cap and halving schedule. Block reward split
across miners + secure nodes + super nodes + treasury. No active fee
burn. Returns historically track ZEC/privacy-coin beta plus an EVM-
sidechain narrative discount/premium.
