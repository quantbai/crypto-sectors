## What it is

Sei is a Cosmos-SDK-based L1 launched August 2023, positioned as a
"trading-focused" chain with built-in central limit order book
primitives (since deprecated in favor of EVM compatibility via Sei v2
in 2024). Backed by Multicoin / Jump / Coinbase Ventures.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking yield from inflation, (2) gas fees paid in SEI
with partial allocation to ecosystem incentives, (3) ecosystem subsidies
fund DeFi/perps activity on the chain. Value accrual mechanic is
generic Cosmos-SDK L1 — no aggressive burn like Injective, no
distinctive fee model. Returns largely driven by ecosystem-incentive
flow and narrative cycles.

- **Keep current** sector, sub-group as "Cosmos SDK chains" (with INJ).

But Sei's lack of a distinctive value-accrual mechanic (vs INJ's aggressive burn) means it may co-move more with the SUI/APT cohort on unlock-schedule grounds than with INJ on burn-mechanic grounds.
