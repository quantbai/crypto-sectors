## What it is

XRP is the native asset of the XRP Ledger, a permissioned-validator
consensus chain operated since 2012, marketed primarily for cross-border
payments and FX settlement through Ripple's institutional product suite
(ODL / RippleNet). Post-SEC settlement (2023) it trades as a major-cap
payment-rail token.

## Value accrual mechanism

No staking, no fee burn beyond a tiny anti-spam burn (~10 drops per tx).
Value accrual is almost entirely demand-side: corridor liquidity demand
(Ripple ODL), exchange-driven speculation, and the gradual release of
Ripple-held escrow. Closer to a bearer payment-instrument than a
productive crypto-asset.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- XLM — Stellar, direct fork of pre-2014 XRP code with the same remittance
  thesis (Jed McCaleb founded both)
- LTC — Litecoin, "lighter BTC" payment beta
- BCH — Bitcoin Cash, payment-focused BTC fork
- (Distant) HBAR — enterprise-payments narrative

## Classification verdict

- **Keep current** (102030 Remittance Coins) but pair tightly with XLM
  in any sub-grouping.
- Rationale: XRP and XLM are near-clones in mechanism, founder lineage,
  and narrative. They are the only pure cross-border remittance plays in
  the universe and should form a 2-asset "Payment / Cross-border" sub-group.
  Mixing them with memes or privacy coins in "Specialized Coins" is what
  destroys cohesion.
