## What it is

Shiba Inu is an ERC-20 meme launched in August 2020, framed as a
"DOGE killer". It has since expanded into an ecosystem: ShibaSwap DEX, a
BONE governance token, LEASH companion token, and Shibarium, an L2 on
Ethereum (live 2023).

## Value accrual mechanism

Fixed supply (1 quadrillion minted; ~410 T circulating after Vitalik's
410 T burn). Ongoing burn via Shibarium fees and dedicated burn portals,
but burn rate is low relative to supply. Value is dominated by social
attention and CEX listing flows. Shibarium gas demand is the closest
thing to a productive accrual channel but contributes < 1% of price action.
