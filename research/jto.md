## What it is

Jito is Solana's leading liquid-staking protocol (issuing JitoSOL) and operates the dominant MEV infrastructure on Solana — the Jito-Solana validator client and the Jito Block Engine that handles bundle auctions and tip distribution to validators. Most Solana stake by 2025 routes through Jito-Solana client + Block Engine. JTO is the governance token.

## Value accrual mechanism

JTO governs the JitoSOL fee parameters and the Jito Block Engine tip distribution. JitoSOL earns base staking + MEV rewards (Solana's MEV is meaningful through Jito tips), so JTO indirectly captures a share of the largest MEV business in crypto. Recent governance has implemented JTO buybacks funded by protocol revenue, creating a real-yield link.
