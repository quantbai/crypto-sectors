# ENS — Ethereum Name Service

**asset_id**: `ens`
**Current classification**: sector_code=`App Utilities (4)`, chain_ecosystem=`ETH`
**Current sector cohesion**: -0.018, NEG (parent sector)

## What it is

ENS is the dominant decentralized naming service mapping human-readable
names (`.eth`) to Ethereum addresses, content hashes, and metadata.
Hundreds of wallets and dapps integrate ENS resolution; the de facto
"DNS for crypto."

## Value accrual mechanism

Annual renewal fees on `.eth` names (paid in ETH) flow to the ENS DAO
treasury. ENS token is purely governance — controls fee schedule, treasury
allocation, and protocol upgrades. No direct revenue distribution, no burn,
no staking. Token value is a claim on governance over a fee-generating
naming registry.
