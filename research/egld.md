## What it is

MultiversX (rebrand of Elrond in 2022) is a non-EVM PoS L1 with
adaptive state sharding and the WASM-based Arwen VM. Launched mainnet
2020. Has pivoted through several narratives (sharded L1, metaverse,
SocialFi) without sustained traction.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking yield from inflation (~7%, declining schedule),
(2) transaction fees with a partial burn (most fees recycled to
validators), (3) very limited DeFi/application activity. Value accrual
is dominated by inflation more than fee capture; EGLD return profile
is essentially a long-tail L1 with a retail Romanian/EU holder base
(founder Beniamin Mincu, Sibiu-based team).
