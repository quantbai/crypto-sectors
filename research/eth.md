## What it is

Ethereum is the dominant general-purpose smart-contract platform and the
settlement layer for the majority of on-chain DeFi, stablecoin (USDC, DAI),
restaking, and RWA activity. Largest L1 by TVL and developer count.

## Value accrual mechanism

Three channels: (1) base-fee burn via EIP-1559 (deflationary pressure
proportional to network activity), (2) staking yield from validators
(~3-4% issuance) plus MEV/tips, (3) ETH as collateral / unit of account
across DeFi and L2 fee payment. ETH issuance net of burn is the closest
crypto analogue to a productive cash-flow asset; ETH-beta is roughly the
"DeFi market beta" of the universe.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- SOL — competing general-purpose L1, also fee-burn + staking
- AVAX — EVM-equivalent L1 with similar value-accrual mechanics
- BNB — similar burn mechanic but value dominated by Binance exchange flows
- GNO — Gnosis Chain is ETH-aligned; tracks ETH beta closely
- Restaking governance (EIGEN) — derivative of ETH staking yield
