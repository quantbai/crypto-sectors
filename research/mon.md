## What it is

Monad is a parallel-execution EVM L1, mainnet launched November 24,
2025. Designed to deliver ~10,000 TPS while maintaining full EVM
bytecode compatibility (re-architected execution engine, optimistic
parallel execution, custom MonadDb). Built by Monad Labs (Keone Hon,
Eunice Giarta — ex-Jump Trading). Raised $244M, including $225M
Paradigm-led round.
[monad.xyz/announcements, learn.backpack.exchange/articles/monad-mainnet-launch]

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Validator staking — MON secures the chain, (2) gas fees paid in
MON for all on-chain activity (EVM-compatible), (3) very large 100B
total supply with 7.5% allocated to public sale at $0.025. Tokenomics
are aggressive (large team/investor allocations, multi-year vesting);
value accrual is the generic "EVM L1 fee burn + staking" pattern with
no distinctive twist. Bet is on parallel-EVM throughput attracting
DeFi / consumer-app developer flow from ETH and SOL.
