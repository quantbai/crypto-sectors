## What it is

Dogecoin is a 2013 Litecoin fork that began as a parody but evolved into
the canonical meme-coin and a working payment chain (Scrypt PoW, ~1-min
block time). It is the largest meme by market cap and the only meme with
its own L1.

## Value accrual mechanism

Inflationary issuance: a flat 10,000 DOGE per block (~5 B/year), no
hard cap, no burn, no staking. Value is purely demand-side: social
attention, Elon Musk endorsement cycles, and tipping/merchant payment use.
DOGE behaves as a pure liquidity-driven meme with high BTC-beta.
