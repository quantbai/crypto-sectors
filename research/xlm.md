## What it is

XLM is the native asset of the Stellar network, a permissioned-validator
payments chain founded by Jed McCaleb (also XRP co-founder) in 2014. The
chain is optimized for low-cost FX corridors, stablecoin issuance (USDC
issued natively), and MoneyGram-style cash-in/cash-out integrations.

## Value accrual mechanism

Stellar charges minimal per-tx fees (0.00001 XLM) and has no staking or
burn. Annual 1% inflation was terminated in 2019; supply is now fixed at
50 B with ~30 B held by the Stellar Development Foundation. Value accrual
is demand-side: anchor partnerships, USDC settlement volume, and SDF
treasury management. Soroban smart contracts (live 2024) add a small
gas-demand channel but XLM is structurally a payment-rail token, not a
productive L1.
