# HBAR — Hedera

## What it is

Hedera is a permissioned-validator-set hashgraph DLT (not a blockchain
proper), governed by a council of large enterprises (Google, IBM,
Boeing, LG, etc.). EVM-compatible smart contracts (HTS, HSCS).
Positioned as enterprise-grade rails for tokenized assets and
file/consensus services.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Transaction fees paid in HBAR with a portion of network revenue
flowing to council nodes, (2) node-operator rewards, (3) HTS-based RWA
issuance demand. Value accrual is low and tightly controlled; HBAR's
return profile is driven more by enterprise-narrative pumps (ETF
applications, partnership announcements) than fundamental network usage.
