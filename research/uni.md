## What it is

Uniswap is the largest AMM-based spot DEX, originally on Ethereum and now deployed across many EVM chains (Arbitrum, Base, Polygon, Optimism, BNB, etc.). It pioneered the constant-product (x*y=k) liquidity-pool model in v2, concentrated liquidity in v3, and "hooks" in v4. UNI is the protocol's governance token.

## Value accrual mechanism

UNI is primarily a governance token; the legendary "fee switch" ‚Äî turning on a protocol-level cut of swap fees that would flow to UNI stakers/holders ‚Äî has been debated for years but largely remains off (limited deployments). Today UNI has no direct cash-flow accrual; valuation rests on optionality over future fee switch activation, governance influence over the largest DEX, and ecosystem capture as Uniswap Labs ships front-ends and L2s (Unichain).
