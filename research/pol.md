## What it is

POL is the native asset of Polygon, replacing MATIC 1:1 on September 4,
2024. Polygon comprises: (a) Polygon PoS (the original sidechain, ~7M tx/day),
(b) Polygon zkEVM, and (c) AggLayer, a cross-chain settlement layer that
aggregates Polygon-connected chains.

## Value accrual mechanism

POL is "hyperproductive": stakers secure multiple Polygon chains
simultaneously and earn fees from each. Channels: (1) Polygon PoS gas
fees, (2) AggLayer settlement fees (cross-chain interop), (3) staking
emissions (currently ~2% inflation). The AggLayer thesis is the new
value-accrual story ŌĆö fees from any AggLayer-connected chain flow to POL
stakers. Adoption is the open question.
