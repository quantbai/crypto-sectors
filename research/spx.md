## What it is

SPX6900 is a satirical ERC-20 meme launched 2024, parodying the S&P 500
under the slogan "6900 is more than 500". Marketed as a "stock market for
the people" with a stated $69 T target market cap. Multi-chain (ETH,
Solana, Base via Wormhole bridging) but ETH is the canonical deploy.

## Value accrual mechanism

Fixed supply 1 B (6.9% burned at launch, mint authority renounced). No
staking, no burn, no fee revenue. Pure attention-driven meme economics —
the AEON NFT collection adds a tangential collectibles narrative but
contributes nothing to token value accrual.
