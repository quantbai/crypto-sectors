## What it is

IOTA is a feeless DAG-based ("Tangle") distributed ledger aimed originally
at IoT machine-to-machine payments. Migrated to a more standard L1
("Stardust" 2023, "Rebased" / IOTA 2.0 in 2025 using a Move-based L1).
Long history but limited application traction.

## Value accrual mechanism

After Rebased, IOTA L1 uses staking (PoS) — validators stake IOTA, earn
inflation. Network fees (post-feeless era) are paid in IOTA. Demand
drivers: enterprise pilots (TLIP shipping, EU partnerships), IoT data
markets. Heavy reliance on partnership-narrative; weak fee revenue.
