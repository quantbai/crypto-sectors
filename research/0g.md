## What it is

Zero Gravity (0G) is a modular AI-focused L1 with integrated
decentralized storage, compute, and data-availability layers. Aristotle
mainnet launched September 17-22, 2025. Pitched as the infrastructure
layer for on-chain AI models / inference / agents. 0G Labs claims to
have trained a 107B-parameter decentralized AI model in 2025.
[0g.ai, cryptonomist.ch/2025/09/22/0g-labs-launches-aristotle-ai-mainnet-live-token-debut]

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) 0G token pays for transaction execution, AI inference compute,
and data storage on the network (multi-use gas/utility), (2)
validator staking, (3) demand coupling with AI workload growth — if
AI-agent / on-chain-inference adoption scales, storage/compute demand
translates to 0G burn. Value accrual is structurally interesting
(unit-of-work pricing for AI compute) but speculative; current
on-chain AI activity is narrative-driven more than usage-driven.
