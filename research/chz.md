## What it is

Chiliz is the protocol token of the Socios.com fan-token platform — a
permissioned-validator EVM-equivalent chain ("Chiliz Chain") issuing
licensed fan tokens for major sports clubs (PSG, Barcelona, Juventus,
Inter, Manchester City, F1 teams, UFC, etc.).

## Value accrual mechanism

CHZ is the gas + reserve currency for Chiliz Chain. New fan tokens are
launched via Chiliz "Fan Token Offerings" priced in CHZ, creating direct
CHZ demand. Validator staking generates yield from gas fees. Returns
driven by sports-season cycles, major club partnerships, and licensing
deals; relatively independent from general crypto beta.
