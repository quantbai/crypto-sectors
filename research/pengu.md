## What it is

PENGU is the ecosystem token of the Pudgy Penguins NFT brand, launched
on Solana on December 17, 2024. Supply 88.88 B (25.9% airdropped to
Pudgy Penguins / Lil Pudgys / Rogs holders). Pudgy Penguins is a leading
non-degen NFT brand with mainstream merch presence (Walmart, Target).

## Value accrual mechanism

No staking, no burn, no protocol fees flowing to PENGU. Token is
positioned as ecosystem governance + access for Pudgy IP / future Abstract
L2 (Igloo Inc.) games. Real-world brand merchandising creates some demand
narrative but no contractual revenue flow to PENGU. Behaves as a meme +
NFT-ecosystem hybrid.
