## What it is

Starknet is StarkWare's permissionless ZK-rollup on Ethereum, using
zk-STARK proofs and the Cairo VM (non-EVM-equivalent but smart-contract-
capable). STRK launched February 2024 via airdrop.

## Value accrual mechanism

10 B total supply with multi-year unlock. As of v0.14.0 (September 2025),
transaction fees on Starknet are paid only in STRK (sequencers may
convert to ETH for L1 gas). STRK staking secures sequencing, proving,
and DA services — partial PoS economics. Governance is on STRK. So STRK
has more direct mechanism-level accrual than ARB/OP, but utilization is
still ramping.
