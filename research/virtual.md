## What it is

Virtuals Protocol is a decentralized AI-agent launchpad and ownership
layer on Base (ETH L2). Anyone can create an AI agent, tokenize it via
bonding curve, and share its economic activity. Launched Oct 2024.
Source: [Coin Bureau](https://coinbureau.com/review/virtuals-protocol-review).

## Value accrual mechanism

VIRTUAL is the pairing/quote asset for every agent token launch on the
platform (every agent's bonding curve is denominated in VIRTUAL). Protocol
fees from agent trading and creation accrue to VIRTUAL holders/treasury.
Agent-economy demand → VIRTUAL demand. Effectively a "Uniswap-LP-style"
token for the agent launchpad — value scales with agent-launch volume
and revenue capture.
