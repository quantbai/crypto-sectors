## What it is

Ondo Finance is the largest tokenized-treasuries issuer in crypto. Flagship products include USDY (yield-bearing dollar backed by short-duration US Treasuries) and OUSG (tokenized BlackRock BUIDL / treasury fund access for institutions). Ondo also operates Ondo Chain (RWA-focused L1 with institutional validators) and Ondo Global Markets (tokenized US equities). Deployed across Ethereum, Solana, Sui, Aptos, and Mantle.

## Value accrual mechanism

ONDO is the governance token of the Ondo DAO and Ondo Foundation. It controls Ondo Chain validator set, protocol parameters, and treasury. Cash flows from RWA management fees accrue to the Foundation rather than directly to token holders today, but the token serves as long-dated optionality on tokenized-treasury and tokenized-equity scale + Ondo Chain gas economics.
