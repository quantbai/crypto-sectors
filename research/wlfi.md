## What it is

World Liberty Financial is a Trump-family-affiliated DeFi platform founded in 2024 (founders: Folkman, Herro, Witkoff brothers, plus Trump family members in advisory/branding roles). Its primary product is USD1 — a fiat-backed dollar stablecoin (cash + short-duration treasuries, custodied via BitGo Trust). The protocol also operates a lending/borrowing front-end built on Aave v3 infrastructure. WLFI became transferable on 1 Sept 2025 after a governance vote [CoinGecko explainer](https://www.coingecko.com/learn/what-is-world-liberty-financial-donald-trump-s-crypto-project).

## Value accrual mechanism

WLFI is the governance token. Stated future utilities include governance, possible fee revenue sharing from lending operations, staking rewards, and discounted transaction fees. Today value capture is governance + narrative; ~80% of supply remains locked. Trump entities receive 75% of net WLFI token sale proceeds plus a cut of stablecoin profits.

## Economic siblings

Mechanically: stablecoin issuer (USD1 ≈ USDT/USDC analog) + lending front-end (Aave wrapper). Closest siblings: SKY, ENA, RSR (stablecoin issuers); also adjacent to AAVE (uses Aave back-end). Behavior is dominated by political/narrative flow rather than DeFi mechanics.
