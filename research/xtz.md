## What it is

Tezos is a non-EVM PoS L1 launched 2018 (after a controversial $232M
ICO), known for on-chain governance / self-amendment and the Michelson
smart contract language. Cathedral/bazaar academic origin (Arthur and
Kathleen Breitman). Multiple narrative pivots: governance, NFTs (FX
Hash), then quiet drift.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Baking / endorsing rewards from inflation (~5-6%), (2) transaction
fees paid to bakers (validators) — no burn, (3) on-chain governance
where XTZ holders vote on protocol upgrades. Value accrual is weak —
no burn, low fee revenue, large foundation supply. Returns essentially
"narrative L1 with retail holders" pattern.
