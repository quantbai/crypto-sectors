## What it is

Raydium is one of Solana's earliest and most-used AMMs. It pioneered Serum-orderbook-integrated AMM pools (now using its own CLMM after Serum's collapse) and has been a core liquidity venue for Solana memecoin trading. Through 2024ŌĆō2025, Raydium captured a large share of memecoin-graduation flow from Pump.fun until Pump.fun launched its own PumpSwap AMM.

## Value accrual mechanism

A portion of swap fees is used to buy back and burn RAY (deflationary mechanism). RAY also gates governance and is used for liquidity-pool staking incentives. Real-yield linkage is meaningful given Solana memecoin volume; revenue is volatile and tied to Solana retail-trading cycles.
