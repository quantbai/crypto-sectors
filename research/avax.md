## What it is

Avalanche is an EVM-equivalent L1 with the C-Chain as the main DeFi
environment, plus a "subnet" / "L1" architecture (post-Avalanche9000
upgrade, late 2024) allowing application-specific chains to launch with
AVAX as the staking asset.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Base transaction fees on C-Chain are burned (EIP-1559-style),
(2) validator staking yield from inflation, (3) subnets/L1s pay
continuous AVAX fees for validation, creating a recurring demand stream
post-9000. Value accrual mechanics very close to ETH but with smaller
absolute fee revenue.
