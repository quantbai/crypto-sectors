## What it is

Render is a decentralized GPU rendering / compute network — originally
for 3D/VFX workloads (OctaneRender), now expanding to AI inference. Node
operators contribute idle GPU capacity; jobs are paid in RENDER. Migrated
from ETH to Solana in 2023 for lower fees.

## Value accrual mechanism

RENDER is the medium of exchange for GPU compute jobs. "Burn-and-mint
equilibrium" (BME): users burn RENDER to pay for work; protocol mints new
RENDER to pay node operators. Net inflation/deflation depends on whether
job demand exceeds emission. Fee-burn captures usage growth directly.
