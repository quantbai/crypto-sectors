## What it is

Gala Games is a blockchain gaming platform (2019) with a portfolio of
games (Town Star, Spider Tanks, Mirandus) and the proprietary GalaChain
L1 (migrated off ETH in 2024). Founder's Nodes (50 K node licenses)
participate in block production and receive GALA emissions.

## Value accrual mechanism

Original ERC-20 GALA migrated 1:1 to native GalaChain GALA in 2024. Daily
emissions distributed to Founder's Nodes; emissions schedule halves
periodically. A new 2026 tokenomics vote introduced a disinflationary
fee-burn model. GALA is the gas token for GalaChain — cross-chain NFT
transfers (e.g., Shrapnel from Avalanche) mandate GALA gas, driving
intrinsic demand.
