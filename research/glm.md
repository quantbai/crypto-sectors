## What it is

Golem is one of the oldest decentralized compute marketplaces — providers
rent out CPU/GPU capacity, users pay GLM for compute jobs (rendering,
ML, scientific). Migrated from GNT to GLM token (2020), ERC-20 on Ethereum.
Limited mainstream traction vs newer entrants like Render or Akash.

## Value accrual mechanism

GLM is the medium of payment between requestors and providers. Fixed
supply (~1B), no inflation, no burn — pure utility access token. Value
accrual is bounded by job-volume × token-velocity, which has been weak
historically. No staking/yield mechanic.
