## What it is

Optimism is an optimistic-rollup L2 on Ethereum and the originator of
the OP Stack, which powers the "Superchain" (Base, Worldchain, Mode,
Mantle-EVM, etc.). OP is the governance token of the Optimism Collective
(Token House + Citizens' House dual structure).

## Value accrual mechanism

Superchain OP-Stack chains pay revenue share to the Collective: the
greater of 2.5% of chain revenue or 15% of on-chain profit. As of Feb
2026, governance approved using 50% of net Superchain sequencer revenue
for monthly OP buybacks (~$8 M/yr at current rates). Repurchased OP is
held in treasury тАФ burn or staking-reward distribution is TBD by
governance.
