## What it is

Kamino is Solana's largest decentralized lending and liquidity protocol (~$3.2B TVL by early 2026). Core products: K-Lend (pool-based lending), Earn Vaults (auto-deployed single-sided yield), Multiply Vaults (leveraged farming), and concentrated-liquidity DEX vaults that wrap Orca/Raydium CLMMs [CoinMarketCap](https://coinmarketcap.com/cmc-ai/kamino-finance/what-is/). In 2026 Kamino added tokenized-equity collateral (xStocks via Superstate), shifting toward institutional lending.

## Value accrual mechanism

KMNO is the governance token; staking unlocks boosted rewards and protocol-fee discounts. The protocol earns interest spread + vault management fees, with revenue flowing to the DAO treasury. Airdrop seasons have distributed KMNO to active users; transition to fee-share / buyback economics is under discussion.
