## What it is

Bittensor is a decentralized machine-intelligence network where "subnets"
host specialized AI models (LLM inference, embeddings, prediction, etc.).
Miners produce outputs, validators score them; rewards distributed via
Yuma Consensus. Operates its own L1 (substrate-based, Bitcoin-like
halvings).

## Value accrual mechanism

TAO follows a Bitcoin-style emission curve (~21M cap, halvings ~every 4y).
Miners and validators earn block rewards for AI work; subnets pay TAO to
register. dTAO (dynamic subnet token model, late 2024) lets each subnet
mint its own subtoken priced in TAO. No external fee burn; value is
narrative-driven (AI compute) plus emission-curve scarcity.
