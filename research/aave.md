## What it is

Aave is the largest pool-based decentralized lending protocol, deployed across Ethereum mainnet, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Polygon, Avalanche, and others. Users deposit collateral, borrow against it at variable or stable rates, and earn supply yield. v3 introduced isolation/efficiency modes, and the protocol also issues GHO, an over-collateralized stablecoin.

## Value accrual mechanism

AAVE holders govern the protocol and can stake AAVE in the Safety Module to backstop bad debt in exchange for protocol incentives (a slashing-style insurance backstop). Protocol revenue (interest spread + GHO interest + flashloan fees) accrues to the DAO treasury; the "Aavenomics" upgrade (Umbrella + buyback) routes a portion of revenue to AAVE buybacks/staker rewards. Value driver: lending-volume cycle and stablecoin GHO scale.

## Economic siblings

Other pool-based lending protocols: COMP (Compound), MORPHO (Morpho), FLUID (Fluid), JST (Just Lend, TRON), KMNO (Kamino, Solana). Stablecoin-issuer overlap via GHO (similar to SKY/Maker, but GHO is secondary product).
