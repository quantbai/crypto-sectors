## What it is

Decentraland is an ETH-based 3D virtual world (2020) with parcel-based
LAND NFTs and an active DAO-governance model. MANA is the ERC-20
in-world currency.

## Value accrual mechanism

MANA is burned when used to purchase LAND/items from the primary market
(early bonding mechanic). Secondary marketplace fees route to DAO
treasury. No staking. ~2.2 B supply, declining slowly via burn. Returns
driven by metaverse-narrative cycle, brand activations (corporate land
purchases), and dilution from team unlocks.
