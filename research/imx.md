## What it is

Immutable is a gaming-focused ZK-rollup on Ethereum (StarkEx-based
Immutable X, plus newer Immutable zkEVM in partnership with Polygon).
Specializes in NFT minting/trading for games (Gods Unchained, Illuvium,
Guild of Guardians). Launched IMX token in late 2021.

## Value accrual mechanism

2 B fixed supply. IMX is used for: (1) Immutable X marketplace
transaction fees (20% of fees paid in IMX), (2) staking (rewards
distributed every 14 days from the fee pool + emissions), (3) governance.
Burn occurs via the protocol fee distribution mechanism. Returns driven
by gaming-sector adoption (Web3 game launches) and ETH-L2 narrative.
