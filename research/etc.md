## What it is

Ethereum Classic is the original ETH chain following the 2016 DAO-hack
hard fork (which created the canonical ETH). PoW (Ethash, the only
remaining significant Ethash chain after ETH's 2022 Merge). EVM-
equivalent but with effectively no active developer ecosystem or DeFi.

## Value accrual mechanism

(1) Miner rewards from block subsidy (with the 5M20 supply cap and
1/5 emission reduction every 5M blocks), (2) trivial transaction fee
revenue, (3) speculative beta to ETH narrative + speculative miner-
migration flow after ETH's PoS transition. No fee burn. Value accrual
is effectively zero; ETC is a PoW miner-collateral / "ETH on PoW"
narrative trade.

## Economic siblings (most similar tokens in our universe)

- BTC, LTC — PoW miner-collateral assets (though these are in 1010 Value Transfer)
- Within SCP: no good economic sibling — ETC is anomalously a PoW SCP

## Classification verdict

- **Reclassify candidate** — ETC's economic behavior is closer to PoW Value Transfer Coins (BTC/LTC cohort) than to active SCPs. But ETC is technically EVM-capable, so the current sector tag is defensible.
- Suggested sub-group within SCP: "PoW / orphaned SCPs" (singleton, plus possibly NEO/QTUM legacy).
- Rationale: ETC has zero meaningful application activity. Return profile is dominated by ETH-narrative spillover and miner economics — better captured by a beta-to-ETH factor than by SCP cohort membership.
