## What it is

FLOKI is a meme token (ERC-20 + BEP-20 dual-deploy) named after Elon
Musk's Shiba Inu dog and launched in 2021. The project has tried to bolt
on utility (Valhalla MMORPG, FlokiFi token-locker, TokenFi tokenization)
but remains return-driven by meme attention.

## Value accrual mechanism

Fixed supply (~10 T). Modest burns via marketing-tax mechanic on some
DEX trades (~0.3%). Valhalla and TokenFi adoption could in principle drive
FLOKI demand, but volumes are negligible. Returns continue to be
dominated by meme-cycle social attention, often correlated with DOGE/SHIB.
