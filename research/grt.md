## What it is

The Graph is a decentralized indexing and query protocol for blockchain
data — dapps publish "subgraphs," indexers serve queries. Powers data
layers for Uniswap, Aave, ENS, and most major dapps. Expanded into
"Substreams" and Firehose data services.

## Value accrual mechanism

Consumers pay GRT as query fees to indexers; indexers stake GRT as
collateral and earn fees + inflation rewards. Curators stake to signal
quality subgraphs; delegators delegate to indexers. GRT inflation funds
indexer rewards (~3% target). Burn portion of query fees. Value accrual
depends on paid-query volume growth vs ongoing inflation.
