## What it is

The Sandbox is a voxel-based virtual world (think Roblox + Minecraft on
ETH) where users own LAND NFTs and build/monetize experiences. SAND is
the ERC-20 utility/governance token, launched 2020 and a key beneficiary
of the late-2021 metaverse narrative cycle.

## Value accrual mechanism

3 B fixed supply. SAND is used to buy LAND, items, and experiences;
LAND sales by The Sandbox studio recycle SAND back to treasury. Staking
SAND earns emissions plus rewards from a fee-share pool. Burn occurs
via 5% transaction tax on marketplace volume. Real driver is LAND-sale
primary issuance plus secondary-market royalties — both heavily cyclical
and currently depressed.
