## What it is

Pepe is an ERC-20 meme launched April 2023 referencing the Pepe the Frog
internet character. It is a pure meme — no native chain, no DeFi or
utility layer, no staking. Famous for the 2023 vertical-launch rally that
defined the post-Shanghai memecoin season.

## Value accrual mechanism

Fixed supply (~420 T after team-vault burn). No staking, no burn, no
revenue. Value is 100% demand-side: social-attention cycles, CEX listings
(Binance May-2023 added 75% in a day), and reflexive narrative trading.
PEPE is the cleanest "pure-meme ETH-resident" beta in the universe.
