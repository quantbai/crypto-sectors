# LINK — Chainlink

**asset_id**: `link`
**Current classification**: sector_code=`App Utilities (4)`, chain_ecosystem=`ETH`
**Current sector cohesion**: -0.018, NEG (parent sector)

## What it is

Chainlink is the dominant decentralized oracle network, providing off-chain
price feeds, VRF randomness, proof-of-reserve, and CCIP cross-chain messaging
to smart-contract platforms. Secures the majority of DeFi TVL via price oracles.

## Value accrual mechanism

LINK is paid by data consumers (DeFi protocols, RWA issuers) to node operators
who provide feeds; staking (v0.2) requires LINK as security collateral with
slashing for misreports. Fees from CCIP and emerging services (Data Streams,
Functions) accrue to the Build/Scale economic-abstraction model. LINK has
no burn, so value accrual is dominated by usage demand × staking lockup.

- **Reclassify** to "Oracles" sub-group with PYTH.
- Rationale: LINK's economic driver is oracle-fee demand and CCIP volume,
  not generic "application utility." Pairs cleanly with PYTH as oracle
  duopoly; both gain from DeFi/RWA growth and lose to oracle-free designs.
