## What it is

AXS is the governance token of Axie Infinity, the Vietnamese-built
play-to-earn (P2E) creature-battle game from Sky Mavis. It pioneered
P2E in 2021 with the Philippines scholarship economy boom and the
Ronin sidechain (an Ethereum-validator chain).

## Value accrual mechanism

Capped 270 M supply, ~75% circulating. Staking yields AXS emissions
(currently ~5-10% APR depending on lock); historically funded by treasury
emissions, not fee revenue. Ronin chain transaction fees do not flow to
AXS holders; RON is the gas token. Real value driver is in-game demand
for Axie breeding (which burns AXS) — that demand collapsed 2022 and has
not recovered.
